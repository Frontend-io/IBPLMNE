BPM in brief
BPM is a America/Europe leader in global banking and financial services and one of the six strongest banks in the world according to Standard & Poor's. Present across EUR through all its business lines, the Group has four domestic retail banking markets in US, Italy, Belgium and UK. It has one of the largest international networks with operations in 84 countries and 200,900 employees, including 158,700 in EUR, 15,000 in North America and 10,400 in Asia.
