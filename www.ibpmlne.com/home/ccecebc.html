Benefits of the Personal Bank Account

A way to bank free of maintenance and transaction fees*

When we open a personal Current Account for you we will give you an BPM Debit Card and register you for BPM Phone & Internet Banking.

To qualify to bank free of maintenance and transaction fees, all you need to do each fee quarter is to make a purchase with your BPM Debit Card AND use BPM Phone & Internet Banking to make a debit transaction like paying a bill. Please refer to our Related Information section for more details.

Your BPM Debit Card

Your BPM Debit Card is widely accepted at supermarkets, petrol stations, department stores and other retail outlets. It is also accepted world wide in over 120 countries at millions of retail outlets and ATMs where the Maestro and Link symbols are displayed. You can also use your card to pay for goods and services by phone and over the internet where Laser is accepted.

BPM Phone & Internet Banking

With BPM Phone & Internet Banking you have access to your accounts at a time and a place that is convenient to you. You can check the balance on your account, pay a range of bills, transfer money to any account in the Republic of Ireland and abroad, top up a mobile, order a statement and do much more.

Regular Statements

Easy-to-read personal current account statements, listing all your account transactions in $os (you can also get this information anytime through BPM Phone & Internet Banking).

Overdraft facility

Arranging an overdraft can help you get through those times when you run out of cash unexpectedly. It allows you to access additional funds on your account up to an agreed figure. Overdrafts are subject to terms, conditions and financial status and are not available to persons under 18 years of age.

Up to 1,000 more places to bank with BPM Bank

BPM and an Post can make your banking more convenient than ever. You can do a significant part of your banking at your local Post Office.

Please view our Related Information section for more details.

Exemption from fees and charges

There is no change to the existing free maintenance and transaction banking arrangements for customers with the following account types: Online Overdraft, Gain, Masterplan, Student Accounts and Graduate Accounts. You may also be exempt from fees and charges* if you have an BPM Debit Card and are registered for BPM Phone & Internet Banking.

*Other charges still apply. Please see our booklet 'A Guide to Fees and Charges for Personal Current Accounts.' This offer is available until further notice.

For the purpose of earning credit interest, a month means the period starting on day 16 of each month and ending on day 15 of the following month. When day 15 occurs on a weekend or public holiday the monthly period will end on the previous working day and the start date for accrual of credit interest for the next month will be the next calendar day.
