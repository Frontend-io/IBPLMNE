BPM Bank Bank Italy
BPM is a merger between Banco Popolare and Banca Popolare di Milano. These banks had their presence in Italy for years and Milano is the bank's first global headquarters. Along with Banco BPM SpA was founded BPM SpA , the Group's new retail bank which gathers the entire network of branches of Banca Popolare di Milano , consisting of around 700 branches located throughout the country. It had a gross profit for 2015 of TRY 3.023 billion and EUR 6.8 billion in Tier I Capital, as defined by Basel's Bank for International Settlements.

Our clients are Corporate, institutional, commodities, market making and broking entities.
