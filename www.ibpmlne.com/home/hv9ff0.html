Foundation

A Historic Mission

From 1 January 2017 born Banco BPM SpA , the third Italian Banking Group, thanks to the merger between Banco Popolare and Banca Popolare di Milano .

Along with Banco BPM SpA was founded BPM SpA , the Group's new retail bank which gathers the entire network of branches of Banca Popolare di Milano , consisting of around 700 branches located throughout the country.

The new company, which is based in Milan and responds to the parent company Banco BPM, is characterized by operational and commercial aspects arising from the ongoing dialogue with the territory and the community reference. This BPM SpA wants to prove each day "alongside the customer", identifying dynamic solutions, declined according to changing needs.

Aware of the importance of supporting its customers in different periods of life, BPM Spa chooses and promotes - today as yesterday - a savings culture anchored on clear and transparent values, without ever forgetting that the "needs of today's" must quickly find a response so that we can look with confidence to the future..

A Bold Untertaking

The birth of a new country depended heavily on the presence of banking activities nation wide, the drive for industrial development, animating national savings, financing fundamental economic breakthroughs and the means to meet financial borrowings. The aftermath of World War I culminated in a wide array of progress, including financial services which soon took off with an accelerated pace leading to technological advances and the designation of previously unheard methods and criteria governing business. Italy was to suffer deprivation from such innovations and lacked qualified and skilled human resources. BPM began operating at a time of such economic strain.
