In Detail

An Offshore  Bank Account is usually the starting point for customers with international or Offshore banking requirements. You can use your Offshore  Bank Account to manage your day-to-day finances from anywhere in the world.

Competitive interest rates
Interest on all our Offshore accounts is paid without the deduction of tax*.

24 hour internet and phone banking Our internet and phone banking services allow you to bank with us anytime, from anywhere.

Register for Internet Banking to:

  • View balances and statements for all your Offshore accounts
  • Enjoy reduced rates on international money transfers
  • Make free bill payments to UK beneficiaries

Visa debit card in sterling/US dollars, and charge card in $o Debit and credit cards offer a convenient, secure way of accessing your Offshore money, wherever you are.

We offer Visa debit cards in sterling or US dollars and charge cards in $o through American Express . You can apply for a $o American Express Card once you are a customer of BPM International.

Register your card for extra security when shopping online For extra security when shopping online, our customers can register their sterling debit card for online shopping with the MasterCard ®SecureCode™ service or Verified by Visa® service. Please note: Customers holding US dollar Visa debit cards will not be able to register their cards for the Verified by Visa service.

†All interest on BPM International's Offshore accounts is currently paid gross. However, if you are residing in the EU, credit interest may be subject to a retention tax. As an alternative, you can opt to have your account details reported ultimately to your local tax authority.

**There is a £25^ monthly fee for our Offshore customers. However, if your Relationship Balance is £25,000* or more you will be eligible for our Offshore Advance service, available for a monthly fee of £15^. Alternatively choose Offshore Premier, our exclusive service with no monthly fee, available to you if your balance is £60,000* or more.

**A monthly underfunding fee is payable for Offshore Advance and Offshore Premier customers in any given month where your Relationship Balance falls below the stated minimum. The monthly underfunding fee in addition to the monthly fee for Offshore Advance is £10^. The underfunding fee for Offshore Premier is £35^.

*or currency equivalent based on savings held with us or investments arranged through us.
