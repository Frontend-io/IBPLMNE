Personal Loans

Your car is rattling, you're sick of the 1970's kitchen cabinets, or your little baby girl has become a blushing bride overnight. For whatever reason you might need some extra cash, an BPM Personal Loan can help.

Applying online is simple, please see our Next Steps section.

How much can I borrow?

The amount you can borrow is based on your capacity to make regular repayments. It's also important not to overstretch your finances and to take into consideration the possibility of future out-of-the-blue expenses.

How much a Personal Loan costs

The rate offered on a Personal Loan is determined by the amount of the loan. The amount of your loan repayments will depend on the amount you borrow and the period of time you want to take to repay it.

Available for any purpose

For example; holidays, wedding expenses, children's education, or a new kitchen.

Competitive interest rates

You can avail of a competitive variable interest rate. Interest is charged on the reducing balance. View our Personal Lending interest rates in the Related Information section.

Flexible terms*

Very flexible repayment terms from 1 to 5 years, with a frequency to suit your income.

Payment holidays*

You can defer repayments by arrangement e.g. take a break from your repayments at Christmas or summer holiday time.

*The above repayment options may impact on the amount and/or term of the loan
